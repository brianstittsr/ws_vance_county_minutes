# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - FY 2015-16 Budget Discussion

**Date:** June 1, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened to discuss and adopt the FY 2015-16 Budget Ordinance. Public input was received, highlighting concerns over budget allocations, particularly in education and non-profit funding. The budget was adopted without a tax increase, maintaining the current rates for property, fire, and solid waste fees. Key budget adjustments included allocations for health department operations, employee COLA, and building maintenance.

## Key Decisions

- Adoption of the FY 2015-16 Budget Ordinance
- No tax increase for the fiscal year

## Action Items

- [ ] Implement a 2% COLA for county employees effective October 1, 2015
- [ ] Hire an IT Assistant effective January 1, 2016

## Topics Discussed

- FY 2015-16 Budget
- Public education funding
- Insurance for Fire/EMS personnel

## Budget & Financial Matters

- No tax increase; rate remains at 79.2¢
- Fire tax remains at 4¢
- Solid waste fee remains at $105.00 per household
- Additional allocations for health department and building maintenance
- Decreases in health insurance renewal and jail debt refinancing

## Ordinances & Resolutions
