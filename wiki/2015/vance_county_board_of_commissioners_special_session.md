# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** September 28, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address several key issues, including the Neighborhood Stabilization Program, a request from the ABC Board to use excess profits, and the sale of REO property. The board also discussed procedures to fill a board vacancy and announced new appointments for the Fire Chief and EMS Director positions. A closed session was held to discuss a personnel matter.

## Key Decisions

- Authorized the sale of 661 Charles Street under the Neighborhood Stabilization Program.
- Approved the ABC Board's request to apply $200,000 in excess profits to the loan on the ABC Store.
- Authorized the upset bid process for the sale of 152 South Bullock Street.
