# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Personnel Matter

**Date:** March 30, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session on March 30, 2015, at 5:00 a.m. to discuss a personnel matter. The meeting took place in the Commissioners’ Conference Room at the Vance County Administration Building. The session was attended by Chairman Archie B. Taylor, Jr., and Commissioners Gordon Wilder, Deborah F. Brown, Dan Brummitt, Terry E. Garrison, and Thomas S. Hester, Jr., with Commissioner Eddie L. Wright absent. Interim County Manager Robert M. Murphy and Human Resources Director Argretta Johen were also present. The board entered a closed session to discuss the personnel issue and adjourned at 5:30 p.m.
