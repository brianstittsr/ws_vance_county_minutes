# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** March 12, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss several key issues, including the extension of a bond order for water bonds, a heating system for the Farmers Market, and a community market garden project. The board also established goals for FY 2015-16 and discussed solar farm regulations. The meeting concluded with a closed session on a personnel matter.

## Key Decisions

- Extended the bond order for $27,000,000 water bonds from seven to ten years.
- Approved the Farmers Market heating system project with Gupton Services for up to $20,000.
- Supported the concept of a community market garden project without financial obligations.
- Approved nine goals for FY 2015-16.
- Referred solar farm regulations to the Planning/Environmental Committee.

## Action Items
