# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Zoning Amendments and Emergency Dispatch Certification

**Date:** April 6, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss amendments to the zoning ordinance, focusing on solar farms, cemeteries, and manufacturing uses. Public input was received on the proposed changes, with concerns about setbacks and decommissioning of solar farms. The Board also recognized the Vance County E-911 Center's achievements in emergency dispatch certification.

## Key Decisions

- Proposed zoning ordinance amendments were discussed but not finalized.
- Recognition of Vance County E-911 Center's certification achievements.

## Action Items

- [ ] Final version of zoning amendments to be reviewed by the Planning/Environmental Committee.
- [ ] Consideration of a moratorium on solar farms until guidelines are finalized.

## Topics Discussed

- Zoning ordinance amendments
- Solar farms regulations
- Emergency dispatch certification

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Amendment to the Vance County Zoning Ordinance for solar farms and other uses

## Public Comments

- Brian Alsop suggested more stringent requirements for solar farm glare issues.
- John Davis and David York expressed concerns about restrictive setbacks for solar farms.
- Dan Weldon opposed solar farms due to potential property value impacts.
- Frank Hester supported solar farms with specific setback conditions.
