# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Personnel Matters

**Date:** May 26, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session on May 26, 2015, primarily to discuss personnel matters in a closed session. The meeting was attended by six commissioners and key staff members. A significant decision was made to amend the interim county manager's contract. The meeting concluded shortly after the open session resumed.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss personnel matters
- Amended the interim county manager’s contract

## Action Items

- [ ] Amendment of the interim county manager’s contract
