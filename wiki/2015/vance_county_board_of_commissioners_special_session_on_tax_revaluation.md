# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Tax Revaluation

**Date:** December 16, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to adopt the Schedule of Values for the 2016 Tax Revaluation. The meeting included discussions on the ability to amend the Schedule of Values and the role of the Board of Equalization and Review in correcting errors. The Board unanimously adopted the Schedule of Values and discussed the importance of public education during the revaluation process.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted the 2016 Tax Revaluation Schedule of Values

## Action Items

- [ ] Mail new values to residents on February 16, 2015
- [ ] Consider offering educational meetings to the public during the revaluation process

## Topics Discussed

- 2016 Tax Revaluation Schedule of Values
- Public education during revaluation
