# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 8, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss various community issues, including zoning ordinance amendments for solar farms, senior advocacy, and energy savings projects for schools. Key decisions included approving setbacks for solar farms and increasing permit fees. Updates on the Summer Work Program and Water District operations were also provided.

## Key Decisions

- Approved 100 foot front and 50 foot side and rear setbacks for solar farms.
- Increased solar farm conditional use permit fee from $250 to $400.
- Approved resolution supporting the Schools’ Energy Savings Project.

## Action Items

- [ ] Implement the approved solar farm setbacks and updated fee schedule.
- [ ] Continue the Summer Work Program for rising seniors.
- [ ] Send refund checks to residents not included in the water project.
