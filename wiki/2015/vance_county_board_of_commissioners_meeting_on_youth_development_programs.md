**Date:** Not specified  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners meeting focused on various youth development programs and sponsorships. Key areas included recreation, environmental education, fire safety, and cooperative extension programs. The board also discussed funding allocations for local schools, community colleges, and various community organizations. Emphasis was placed on programs aimed at youth employment, education, and safety.

## Key Decisions

- Continued funding for Vance County Schools and Vance Granville Community College
- Approval of funding for community organizations such as Boys and Girls Club and Central Children’s Home
