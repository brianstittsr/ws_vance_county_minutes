# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Economic Development

**Date:** November 23, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss a legal and economic development matter. The meeting included a closed session to deliberate on extending the job creation deadline for Semprius under the Economic Catalyst Equipment Lease. The board approved a motion to request an extension from the Golden LEAF Foundation, contingent upon Semprius receiving significant investment funds.

## Key Decisions

- Authorized staff to request an extension of the Economic Catalyst Equipment Lease job creation deadline for Semprius until May 1, 2016, with a further extension until May 1, 2017, upon certification of investment funds.
- Authorized the Chairman to execute an amendment to the grant documents with the Golden LEAF Foundation.

## Action Items

- [ ] Staff to submit a request to the Golden LEAF Foundation for deadline extension.
- [ ] Chairman to execute an amendment to the grant documents.

## Topics Discussed

- Legal/economic development matter
- Extension request for Semprius job creation deadline
