# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** November 2, 2015  
**Year:** 2015  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss several key issues including public comments on property tax relief for seniors, the appointment of a new District 5 Commissioner, and updates from the Department of Social Services. The Board also addressed a rezoning request and discussed progress on the county water system project. New appointments for EMS Director and Fire Chief were announced, and committee reports on human resources and planning were reviewed.

## Key Decisions

- Appointed Leo Kelly, Jr. to the District 5 Commission seat.
- Approved rezoning request for 61 Vincent Hoyle Road.
- Approved owner-engineer agreement with LKC Engineering for Phase 3 of the water system.

## Action Items

- [ ] Swearing in ceremony for Leo Kelly, Jr. scheduled for December 7th.
- [ ] Staff to provide additional cost details for Phase 3 water system project.

## Topics Discussed

## Budget & Financial Matters
