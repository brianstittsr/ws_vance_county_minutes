# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Water System Projects

**Date:** August 21, 2018  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss the construction of Phase 3 of the county water system, updates on Phase 1B, and the establishment of base rates for larger water meters. The board awarded the Phase 3 construction contract to H.G. Reynolds, addressed funding shortfalls, and planned for future water system expansions. Additionally, the board discussed legal matters and budget allocations related to the Kittrell Water Association.

## Key Decisions

- Awarded Phase 3 construction contract to H.G. Reynolds for $1,169,557.
- Authorized exploration of additional funding for Phase 1B.
- Established base fees for 3, 4, and 6 inch water meters.
