# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** February 26, 2018  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to receive the FY 2016-17 Audit Report and discuss a legal matter in closed session. The audit, presented by Alan Thompson, revealed no significant findings and highlighted an increase in the county's fund balance and bond ratings. Tax collections improved slightly, and the property valuation was reported. The board commended the county staff for their financial management. The meeting concluded with a closed session to address a legal issue.

## Key Decisions

- Received the FY 2016-17 Audit Report
- Entered into closed session to discuss a legal matter

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Increase in fund balance from $20.5 million to $22.1 million
- Unassigned fund balance increased to $17.4 million
- Tax collections increased from 96.39% to 96.65%
- Total property valuation is $2,716,410,337
- Total levy amount is $24,397,052
