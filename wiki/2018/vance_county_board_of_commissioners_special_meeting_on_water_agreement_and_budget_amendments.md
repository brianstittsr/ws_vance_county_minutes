# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Water Agreement and Budget Amendments

**Date:** July 10, 2018  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss and approve a new water agreement with the City of Henderson, amendments to the City-County Agreement for Joint Funding of the Library, and budget amendments. The new water agreement consolidates the Kittrell Water Association into the Vance County Water District System, maintaining existing water rates and allocations. The meeting also involved convening the Vance County Water District Board for related approvals.

## Key Decisions

- Approved new Water Agreement with the City of Henderson
- Approved 2018 Amendment to City-County Agreement for Joint Funding of the Library
- Approved Budget Amendments
