# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Youth Development Programs

**Date:** Not specified  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners meeting focused on various youth development programs and sponsorships supported by the county. Key departments such as Recreation and Parks, Soil and Water Conservation, Fire Department, Cooperative Extension, and Social Services presented their initiatives. The county also provides significant funding to local schools, community colleges, and organizations like the Boys and Girls Club and the Library, which offer diverse programs for youth engagement and development.

## Topics Discussed

- Youth Development Programs
- Recreation and Parks Initiatives
- Soil and Water Conservation Activities
- Fire Department Youth Programs
- Cooperative Extension Services
- Social Services Programs
- County Funding for Education and Community Organizations
