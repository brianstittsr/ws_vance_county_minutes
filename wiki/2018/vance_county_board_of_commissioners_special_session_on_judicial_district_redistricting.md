# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Judicial District Redistricting

**Date:** January 30, 2018  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss and approve a resolution opposing the proposed redistricting of Vance County from Judicial District 9 to Judicial District 8. The resolution highlighted the strong regional connections between Vance, Granville, Franklin, and Warren Counties, emphasizing shared services and historical ties. The board unanimously approved the resolution, aiming to maintain these connections and ensure effective judicial and law enforcement operations.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of a resolution to keep Vance County in Judicial District 9.
