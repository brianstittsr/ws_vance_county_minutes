# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** October 1, 2018  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened to discuss various county matters including the implementation of new CAD software for emergency operations, the installation of mobile CAD hardware, and the approval of funding for the Vance County Water District Phase 1B project. The board also addressed proposals for vacant school buildings, jail system upgrades, and RV/Camper use regulations. Additionally, resolutions were passed for the sale of county-owned properties and the acceptance of a grant for industrial park development.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of mobile CAD hardware installation with Batteries of NC.
- Approval of the Letter of Conditions for Vance County Water District Phase 1B.
- Approval of jail system upgrades with Cornerstone/Norment Security Group, Inc.
- Acceptance of bids for county-owned properties on Tungsten Mine Road and Elm Street.
- Approval of NC Rural Ready Sites Grant Program resolution.
