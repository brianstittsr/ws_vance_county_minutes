# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** September 10, 2018  
**Year:** 2018  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session on September 10, 2018. Key topics included public safety concerns, zoning ordinance issues, airport authority updates, and water system financing. The board approved untimely tax exemption applications and made an exception to appoint a non-resident to the airport authority. A bond order for water system expansion was also authorized.

## Key Decisions

- Approved untimely tax exemption applications for five properties.
- Appointed Brian Boyd to the Airport Authority for a one-year term.
- Authorized the issuance of water system revenue bonds for Phase 3 expansion.

## Action Items

- [ ] Planning/Environmental Committee to review zoning ordinance regarding RV/camper regulations.

## Topics Discussed

- Public safety and neighborhood watch
- Zoning ordinance on RV/camper regulations
- Henderson-Oxford Airport updates
- 4-H Youth Summit representation
- Water system Phase 3 bond order

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Phase 3 water project funding with over $1,000,000 in USDA grants and $212,000 in revenue bond debt.

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Bond order authorizing the issuance of water system revenue bonds.
