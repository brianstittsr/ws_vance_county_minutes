# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** December 2, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting where new leadership positions were appointed, including Chairperson Deborah F. Brown. Key appointments for county roles were confirmed, and the 2014 holiday schedule was adopted. Updates on the NCFAST Program and appeals for tax exemptions were discussed. Public comments addressed concerns over water fees, and committee reports covered various administrative and community issues.

## Key Decisions

- Deborah F. Brown appointed as Chairperson
- Kelly H. Grissom appointed as Clerk to the Board
- Archie B. Taylor, Jr. appointed as Vice-Chairman
- Jerry L. Ayscue appointed as County Manager
- David C. Beck appointed as Finance Director
- Jonathan S. Care appointed as County Attorney
- 2014 holiday schedule adopted
- Transfer of NSP houses to Franklin-Vance-Warren Opportunities, Inc.
