# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on School Financing

**Date:** May 8, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss financing for public school improvements through Qualified Zone Academy Bonds (QZAB). A resolution was passed to approve an installment financing agreement for up to $2,000,000 to renovate and equip various school facilities. Additionally, the Board approved funding for Northern Vance High School science lab renovations and discussed potential roofing projects pending approval from the Local Government Commission.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution for installment financing agreement for school improvements.
- Approved $364,145 from Clarke Elementary project funds for NVHS Science Labs renovations.
- Authorized use of Schools Capital Outlay Fund for additional NVHS renovation costs.
