# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Water Bond Anticipation Notes

**Date:** January 28, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met in a special session to discuss the issuance of Water Bond Anticipation Notes for Phase 1A of the water project. The Board needed to extend the maturity of existing notes and issue new notes to continue funding the project. A resolution was introduced and passed to authorize the issuance of $3,841,000 in Water Bond Anticipation Notes, with a maturity date set for October 23, 2013.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of a resolution to issue $3,841,000 Water Bond Anticipation Notes.

## Action Items

- [ ] Secure a nine-month extension for the Bond Anticipation Notes.
