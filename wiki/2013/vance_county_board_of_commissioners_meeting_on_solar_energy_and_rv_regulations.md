# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Solar Energy and RV Regulations

**Date:** January 7, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a meeting to discuss amendments to the zoning ordinance, focusing on solar energy systems and the regulation of RVs as long-term residences. The board approved provisions to allow solar collectors and solar farms, promoting sustainable energy within the county. Additionally, they addressed concerns about RVs being used as permanent residences, proposing stricter regulations to prevent this practice.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the ordinance amending the Vance County Zoning Ordinance to add provisions for solar collectors.
- Proposed stricter regulations on the use of RVs as long-term residences.

## Action Items

- [ ] Research removal costs and procedures for obsolete solar farms.
- [ ] Develop regulations to ensure RV compliance with state law.
