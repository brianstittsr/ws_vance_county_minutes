# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - February 2013

**Date:** February 4, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss several key issues, including the recognition of a Veterans Service Officer, the establishment of a No Wake Zone on Kerr Lake, and the introduction of solar farm regulations. The Board also considered the hiring of an environmental manager and the purchase of new technology devices for commissioners. Public input was received on the No Wake Zone proposal, and a resolution was passed to request its implementation. Additionally, the Board approved an ordinance to regulate solar farms within the county.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution for a No Wake Zone in Pughly’s Mill Creek on Kerr Lake.
- Authorized purchase of Lenovo ThinkPad Twist, Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet, or Microsoft Surface Tablet for commissioners.
- Approved Technology Appropriate Use Policy.
- Passed ordinance amending zoning to include solar farms.
