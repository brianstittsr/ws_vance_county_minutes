# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on DSS Director Hiring

**Date:** September 9, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened in a special session to discuss the hiring of a new Director of Social Services. The Board of Social Services had narrowed down the candidates to three individuals, but the desired salary exceeded the budgeted amount. The Commissioners approved an increase in the allowable salary to up to $92,000 to facilitate the hiring process. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Approved an increase in the allowable salary for the DSS Director to up to $92,000.
