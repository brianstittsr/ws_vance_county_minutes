# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Water Project

**Date:** September 26, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss and approve various aspects of the county's water project. Key decisions included approving construction on additional roads for Phase 1A, setting water rates, and awarding construction bids for Phases 2A and 2B. The Board also selected a new engineering firm for future phases. Concerns were raised about the project's financial viability and strategies for increasing customer signups.

## Key Decisions

- Approved construction on additional roads for Phase 1A with change orders totaling $529,347.96.
- Set the minimum base water rate at $30 per customer with a usage rate of $7.19 per 1,000 gallons.
- Awarded construction bids for Phases 2A and 2B to various contractors.
- Selected LKC Engineering as the engineering firm for Phases 2A and 2B.
