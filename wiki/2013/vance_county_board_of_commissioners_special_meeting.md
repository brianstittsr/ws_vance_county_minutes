# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** May 1, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss the FY 2013-2014 Proposed Budget, schedule a budget hearing, and address a resolution on voter identification. The proposed budget maintains current tax rates but recommends a two-cent increase to reduce reliance on the fund balance. A resolution opposing changes to voting requirements was approved with a narrow majority.

## Key Decisions

- Received the FY 2013-2014 Proposed Budget
- Scheduled the Budget Hearing for June 3, 2013
- Approved a resolution opposing changes to voting requirements

## Action Items

- [ ] Commissioners to meet with County Manager and/or Finance Director for budget discussions
- [ ] Distribute the voter resolution to North Carolina legislative delegation

## Topics Discussed

- FY 2013-2014 Proposed Budget
- Budget Hearing Schedule
- Introduction of New Cooperative Extension Director
- Voter Identification Resolution

## Budget & Financial Matters

- No increase in ad valorem or fire tax
- Proposed tax rate remains at 78.2¢
- Solid Waste Household Fee remains at $105.00
- Reduced reliance on fund balance by $100,000
- No employee salary increases
- Capital improvements at Public Health building and Vance Granville Community College
- Four new positions proposed, one position eliminated

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Resolution opposing changes to voting requirements
