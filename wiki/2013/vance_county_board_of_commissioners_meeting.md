# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 3, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss various county programs and initiatives, including the Rural Operating Assistance Program, position vacancies, the Neighborhood Stabilization Program, and school capital fund applications. Key resolutions and motions were passed to support local infrastructure and community development efforts.

## Key Decisions

- Approved submission of the Rural Operating Assistance Program application.
- Authorized filling of position vacancies in Social Services, Jail, and Fire and EMS.
- Empowered the Planning & Environmental Committee to manage the Neighborhood Stabilization Program.
- Approved proclamation for Fall Litter Sweep.
- Approved joint applications for education lottery funds.
- Supported Kerr-Tar Regional Council of Government's grant application for Clean Water Act funds.
- Declared 16 vehicles as surplus and authorized their sale.
