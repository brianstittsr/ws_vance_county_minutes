# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Neighborhood Stabilization Program

**Date:** March 26, 2013  
**Year:** 2013  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to address issues related to the Neighborhood Stabilization Program (NSP). The main focus was on adjusting the listing prices of houses built under the program to make them more affordable for low to moderate income individuals. The board also discussed necessary repairs and security features for these houses to facilitate their sale. The meeting concluded with a unanimous decision to approve the proposed price reductions and repair costs.

## Key Decisions

- Approved reduced list pricing for NSP houses.
- Approved $13,000 for cosmetic repairs and security features.

## Action Items

- [ ] Remove misleading signs about a $20,000 down payment from the houses.
- [ ] Revisit the process if one-third of the houses are not under contract by July.
