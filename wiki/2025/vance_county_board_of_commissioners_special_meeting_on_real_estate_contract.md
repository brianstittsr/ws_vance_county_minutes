# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Real Estate Contract

**Date:** April 28, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened in a special session to discuss and take action on a real estate contract involving the Henderson-Vance Industrial Park. Linde Gas & Equipment, Inc. submitted an offer to purchase three acres adjacent to the shell building tract for $135,000. The Board entered a closed session to deliberate on the contractual matter and subsequently approved the offer with specific conditions. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the offer from Linde Gas & Equipment, Inc. for purchasing three acres at the industrial park for $135,000.

## Action Items
