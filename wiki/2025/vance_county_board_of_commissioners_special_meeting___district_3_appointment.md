# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting - District 3 Appointment

**Date:** February 24, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to appoint a new member to the District 3 Commission seat, previously held by Sean A. Alston, Sr. Four candidates were considered, and after nominations, Charisse Fain was unanimously appointed to the position. The meeting was brief, concluding shortly after the appointment.

## Key Decisions

- Appointment of Charisse Fain to the District 3 Commission seat

## Action Items

- [ ] Charisse Fain to assume the District 3 Commission seat
