# Vance County Board of Commissioners Emergency Closed Session Meeting

**Date:** October 16, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held an emergency closed session meeting to discuss a personnel matter involving a legal action. The meeting took place on October 16, 2025, at the Vance County Administration Building. The primary focus was the settlement of a pending legal action, Sandy Weaver vs. VC DSS Board. After deliberations, the board approved the settlement arrangements.

## Key Decisions

- Entered closed session to discuss the settlement of a legal action related to a personnel matter.
- Approved the settlement arrangements for the case Sandy Weaver vs. VC DSS Board.
