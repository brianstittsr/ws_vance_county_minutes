# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Detention Center

**Date:** April 14, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address issues related to the detention center. Key topics included the approval of the design/build method for construction and repairs, addressing deficiencies highlighted by the Department of Health and Human Services, and implementing an emergency pay policy to tackle staffing shortages. The board also discussed contraband issues and potential solutions, such as random searches. Budget impacts were considered for both the emergency pay policy and pay grade increases for detention staff.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the design/build method for detention center construction and repairs.
- Approved an emergency pay policy for detention services.
- Approved pay grade increases for certain detention center positions.

## Topics Discussed

- Detention center construction and repair methods

- Emergency pay policy for detention staff

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Emergency pay policy impact: $69,000 per month
- Pay grade increase impact: $2,500 per month
