# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Detention Center Plans

**Date:** February 10, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss the future of the county's detention center. Sheriff Curtis Brame and Major William Mitchell emphasized the urgency of deciding between renovating the existing facility or constructing a new one. The board considered various options, including regional collaboration and the financial implications of housing inmates elsewhere. A motion to modify the RFQ with Moseley Architects for both repairs and new construction was passed, despite some opposition.

## Key Decisions

- Motion passed to modify the existing RFQ with Moseley Architects for repairs and new construction of the detention facility.
- Decision to explore regional facility options with Granville County.

## Action Items

- [ ] Major Mitchell to tour Edgecombe County facility for inmate housing during renovations.
- [ ] Public Safety Committee to meet with Granville County representatives about regionalization.
- [ ] County Attorney to address jail health contracts in MOUs with counties housing inmates.

## Topics Discussed

- Detention center renovation or construction
- Inmate housing during renovations
- Regional facility collaboration
- Jail health contracts

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Estimated costs: $5.3 million for repairs, $31.2 million for expansion, $42.5 million for new construction.
- Discussion on costs of housing inmates elsewhere and medical expenses.
