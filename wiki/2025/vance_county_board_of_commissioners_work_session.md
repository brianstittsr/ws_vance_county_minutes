# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session

**Date:** October 20, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss several key issues, including a rezoning case, a proposed telework schedule for the Department of Social Services, revisions to the Fiscal Operations Policy, the establishment of a no-wake zone, and a letter of support for a new detention facility location. No formal decisions were made during the meeting, but several topics were slated for further consideration in upcoming sessions.

## Action Items

- [ ] Submit letter of support for detention facility location
