# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** October 6, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on October 6, 2025. Key agenda items included the appointment of Dywanda Pettaway as Clerk to the Board, recognition of a retiring employee, updates on fire operations, and discussions on project lifesaver and ambulance donation. The board also addressed rezoning requests, flood damage prevention ordinance amendments, and financial reports. Public comments were heard, and several motions were unanimously approved.

## Key Decisions

- Appointed Dywanda Pettaway as Clerk to the Board
- Approved updates to the Background Check Policy
- Approved donation of an ambulance to Vance Granville Community College
- Approved additional funding for IT projects
- Approved flood damage prevention ordinance amendment
- Approved application to draw down lottery funds for ADA compliance upgrades at Carver Elementary School
- Approved proclamation recognizing October 5-11, 2025, as Fire Prevention Week
