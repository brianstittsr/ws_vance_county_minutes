# Vance County Board of Commissioners Budget Work Session

**Date:** May 20, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a budget work session to review and discuss the proposed budget for FY 2025-26. Key topics included tax rates, school budget increases, fire department audit costs, and potential jail construction financing. Discussions also covered infrastructure repairs and salary compression issues. The session concluded with scheduling future meetings and addressing budget process concerns.

## Key Decisions

- No change in tax rate or fire tax rate
- Increase in teacher supplement and classified staff pay
- Consideration of compilation reviews for fire department audits
