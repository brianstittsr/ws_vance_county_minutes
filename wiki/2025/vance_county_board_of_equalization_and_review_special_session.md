# Vance County Board of Equalization and Review Special Session

**Date:** June 17, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Equalization and Review held a special session to address taxpayer appeals regarding property valuations. The meeting was chaired by Carolyn Faines and attended by several commissioners and key staff. The primary focus was on appeals from taxpayers who believed their property values were assessed above fair market value. One appeal was reviewed, where the property value was upheld due to the owner's absence. The meeting concluded with a discussion on the total number of appeals and adjustments made.

## Key Decisions

- Upheld property value of $155,885 for Peter A. Moore's property.
- Approved in-house tax revaluation appeal decisions for June 17, 2025.
