# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** September 22, 2025  
**Year:** 2025  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss several key issues, including a 5% county match for school capital needs grants, an extension of the detention center's emergency pay policy, and the annual meeting for opioid settlement funds. The board approved the local match for the school grant and revised the emergency pay policy despite some disagreement from the Sheriff. Additionally, the board reviewed the use of opioid settlement funds and opened the floor for public comments.

## Key Decisions

- Approved a 5% local match for school capital needs grant applications.
- Approved the revised Detention Center Emergency Pay Policy.
- Held the Opioid Settlement Funds Annual Meeting.

## Action Items

- [ ] County staff to implement the 5% local match for school grants.
- [ ] Finance office to maintain records of emergency pay activity.

## Topics Discussed

- School capital needs grant applications
- Detention Center Emergency Pay Policy
- Opioid Settlement Funds Annual Meeting

## Budget & Financial Matters

- 5% local match for $47.2M school capital needs grant
- Emergency pay policy funding from lapsed salary funds
- Opioid settlement funds received: $2,402,285 to date, $6.4M by 2038
