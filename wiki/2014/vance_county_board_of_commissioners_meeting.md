# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 8, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on September 8, 2014. The meeting included a special recognition for Mrs. Henrietta Clark's 100th birthday, highlighting her contributions to education and community service. The Board also recognized Mr. Timothy M. Daye, Jr. for receiving a leadership scholarship. Ms. Blaire Benson presented the Granville-Vance Faith Initiative for Community Action's efforts in education, which the Board endorsed. Budget amendments and transfers were approved, and public comments on the new County Manager criteria were noted.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution honoring Mrs. Henrietta H. Clark's 100th birthday.
- Endorsed the Granville-Vance Faith Initiative for Community Action's educational efforts.
- Approved budget amendments and transfers.
