# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Property Appeals and Tax Exemption

**Date:** May 27, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address property value appeals and a tax exemption application. The Board of Equalization and Review reconvened to decide on appeals from Kenneth Stevenson regarding properties on Victory Street and Americal Road. A property value reduction was approved for 1148 Victory Street. The Board also considered a late tax exemption application for Joe H. Adcock, Sr., which was approved. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Reduced property value for 1148 Victory Street from $93,737 to $55,000.
- Denied appeal for 389 Americal Road and adjacent lot as the appellant was not the owner as of January 1, 2014.
- Approved late tax exemption application for 987 Carey Chapel Road.
