# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Personnel Matters

**Date:** November 14, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session on November 14, 2014, at the Vance Granville Community College Civic Center in Henderson, NC. The meeting was primarily focused on personnel matters, which were discussed in a closed session. The session began with an invocation by Chairperson Deborah F. Brown and concluded with a motion to adjourn at 11:15 a.m. The meeting was approved and signed on December 1, 2014.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to address personnel matters

## Topics Discussed

- Personnel matters
