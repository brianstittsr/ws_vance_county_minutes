# Vance County Board of Commissioners Regular Meeting

**Date:** February 3, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held their regular meeting on February 3, 2014. Key topics included a public hearing on a rezoning request by Vance-Granville Community College, which was approved unanimously. The meeting also covered pending business related to VFD contracts and audits, and a consent agenda addressing budget transfers and tax refunds. Chairperson Brown recognized various boards and committees for their service.

## Key Decisions

- Approved rezoning request from Light Industrial to Office Institutional for Vance-Granville Community College.
- Accepted VFD contracts and audits report.
- Approved consent agenda items including budget transfers and tax refunds.
