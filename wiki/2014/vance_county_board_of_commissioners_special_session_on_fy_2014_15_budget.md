# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on FY 2014-15 Budget

**Date:** June 23, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened in a special session to address the adoption of the FY 2014-15 budget ordinance, year-end closeouts, property revaluation appraisals, and lease agreements. The budget was approved with a 1¢ property tax increase, despite some opposition. Key budget allocations included new positions in various departments and salary adjustments for employees.

## Key Decisions

- Adoption of FY 2014-15 budget ordinance with a 1¢ property tax increase
- Approval of salary adjustments for employees
- Inclusion of new positions in Social Services, 911, Senior Center, and Planning & Development

## Budget & Financial Matters

- General Fund budget set at $44,153,607
- Total budget including Other Funds is $53,064,344
- Salary adjustments of $804 for full-time and .35¢ per hour for part-time employees
- Health insurance premiums increased by 4.53%
- New positions in various departments
