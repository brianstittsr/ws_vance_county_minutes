# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** June 13, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address the sale of the Armory, approve financing for law enforcement vehicles, and award bids for the CDBG Scattered Site Program. The Armory was sold via auction with proceeds shared with the City of Henderson. Financing terms for purchasing police vehicles were approved, and bids for urgent repairs on three properties were awarded.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution for the sale of the Armory to Kent Davis for $32,500.
- Approved financing terms for the purchase of 10 Ford Police Interceptors.
- Awarded bids for urgent repairs on three properties under the CDBG Scattered Site Program.
