# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Budget Discussion and Public Concerns

**Date:** June 2, 2014  
**Year:** 2014  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss the proposed FY 2014-15 budget. Public input was sought, with concerns raised about tax increases, crime rates, and the efficiency of county services. The Board approved several budget amendments and transfers, and addressed public concerns about county boundary lines.

## Key Decisions

- Approved filling vacancies for Fire Engineer, Fire Specialist, Deputy Sheriff, and Telecommunicator.
- Approved Budget Amendments #16 and #17, and Budget Transfers #21 - #23.
