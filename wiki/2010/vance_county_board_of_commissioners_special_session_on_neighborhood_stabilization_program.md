# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Neighborhood Stabilization Program

**Date:** July 15, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address matters related to the Neighborhood Stabilization Program (NSP). Key discussions included the awarding of demolition and construction bids, acquisition of properties, and authorization of a housing counseling contract. The meeting focused on improving and rehabilitating dilapidated properties to benefit the community.

## Key Decisions

- Awarded demolition bid for 553 and 573 Spring Street to Faulkner Grading for $6,300.
- Awarded demolition bid for 813 Roberson, 1262 Walters, and 1010 Standish to KL Howard Trucking for $18,620.
- Approved purchase of 553 Spring Street for $7,920 and 573 Spring Street for $6,930.
- Awarded construction bid for 553 Spring Street to Humphries Construction for $82,500.
- Authorized contract with Franklin Vance Warren Opportunity for NSP housing counseling not to exceed $36,600.
