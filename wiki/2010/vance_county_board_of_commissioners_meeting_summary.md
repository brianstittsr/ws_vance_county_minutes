# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** January 4, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on January 4, 2010. Key highlights included the recognition of Charles Wilson as the Community Hero for his contributions to local tourism and events. The board received an audit report for FY 2008-09, showing a positive financial position. A lease agreement was approved for Henderson Collegiate Charter School to use county land for mobile classrooms. Updates on urgent repair contracts and the Abandoned Mobile Home Program were also discussed.

## Key Decisions

- Recognition of Charles Wilson as Community Hero
- Approval of FY 08-09 audit report
- Approval of lease agreement with Henderson Collegiate Charter School
- Awarding of urgent repair contracts and demolition bid
