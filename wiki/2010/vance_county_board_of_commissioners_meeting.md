# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 7, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on September 7, 2010. Key topics included the closeout of the Scattered Site Community Development Block Grant Program, recognition of local softball teams for their achievements, and a report from the Division of Community Corrections. Budget amendments were approved, and various administrative matters were addressed.

## Key Decisions

- Closed out the CDBG Scattered Site Program
- Recognized local softball teams for their achievements
- Approved several budget amendments and transfers
