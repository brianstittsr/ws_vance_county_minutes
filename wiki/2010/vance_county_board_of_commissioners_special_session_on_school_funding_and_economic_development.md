# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on School Funding and Economic Development

**Date:** August 23, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened in a special session to approve a funding plan for school projects and discuss an economic development project and a personnel matter. Key decisions included authorizing the sale of Qualified School Construction Bonds (QSCBs) and the use of reserves for school repairs. The board also entered a closed session for further discussions.

## Key Decisions

- Authorize the sale of QSCBs I and II with funding according to fiscal analysis.
- Approve the use of reserves for repairs at the School Central Office.
- Authorize application for an additional $482,730 under QSCB I.

## Action Items

- [ ] Finance Director to proceed with the application for QSCBs.
- [ ] Review funding options for QZAB IV in 6 months.

## Topics Discussed

- School funding plan
- Economic development project
- Personnel matter

## Budget & Financial Matters

- QSCB I: $2,848,100 for Multi-purpose Rooms
- QSCB II: $1,076,220 for Mechanical/HVAC Upgrades
- QZAB IV: $2,000,000 for Repair projects
- Reserves: $403,050 for Central Office repairs
