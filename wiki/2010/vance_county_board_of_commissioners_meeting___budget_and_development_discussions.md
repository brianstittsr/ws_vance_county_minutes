# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Budget and Development Discussions

**Date:** June 7, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on June 7, 2010. Key topics included the proposed FY 2010-11 budget, which maintains current tax rates, and the introduction of the new Economic Development Director, Mr. Guido Essleborn. The Board also discussed potential sites for a new farmer's market and awarded bids for the Neighborhood Stabilization Program. Public comments addressed the condition of local roads and suggestions for community development.

## Key Decisions

- Approved FY 2010-11 budget with no tax increase
- Awarded bids for Neighborhood Stabilization Program projects
- Referred the suggestion for the Armory's use to the Properties Committee
