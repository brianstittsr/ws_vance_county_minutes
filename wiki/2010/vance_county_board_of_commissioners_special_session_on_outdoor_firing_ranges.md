# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Outdoor Firing Ranges

**Date:** September 20, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss the potential enactment of a moratorium on outdoor firing ranges. The meeting included public hearings for citizen input on the moratorium and the development of an ordinance regulating firing ranges. Concerns were raised about safety, noise, environmental impact, and economic implications. The Board considered a 55-day moratorium to allow time for further discussion and ordinance development.

## Key Decisions

- Motion to enact a 55-day moratorium on outdoor firing ranges was passed.
