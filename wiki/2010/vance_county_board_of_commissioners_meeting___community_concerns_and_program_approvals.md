# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Community Concerns and Program Approvals

**Date:** February 8, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session to address various community concerns and approve program procedures. Key discussions included youth engagement, the condition of the animal shelter, and the Community Watch Program. The Board also approved the Abandoned Mobile Home Program Procedures and addressed several budget amendments.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of Abandoned Mobile Home Program Procedures contingent on County Attorney's approval
- Approval of Budget Amendments #14 and #15, Budget Transfer #5, and other financial matters

## Action Items

- [ ] Refer animal shelter concerns to the Animal Control Advisory Committee
- [ ] County Administration to work with Public Safety Committee on employee involvement in Community Watch

## Topics Discussed

- Youth engagement and community activities
- Condition of the Vance County Animal Shelter
- Community Watch Program
- Abandoned Mobile Home Program

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Budget Amendment #14: $7,500 for General Assistance
- Budget Amendment #15: $40,000 for Abandoned Mobile Home Removal Grant
- Budget Transfer #5: $800 for a water fountain

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Resolution declaring 2010 as the Year of Community Watch
