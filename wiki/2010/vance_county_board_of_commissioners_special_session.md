# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** May 20, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened for a special session to receive the FY 2010-2011 Proposed Budget and discuss the Neighborhood Stabilization Program (NSP). The proposed budget maintains current tax rates and does not include salary increases. The Board also discussed acquiring tax-foreclosed properties under the NSP and approved a list of appraisers for these properties.

## Key Decisions

- Received the FY 2010-2011 Proposed Budget.
- Scheduled a Budget Hearing for June 7, 2010.
- Approved the purchase of nine tax-foreclosed properties under the NSP.
- Approved a contract with the City of Henderson for NSP properties, excluding automatic purchases.
- Approved a list of appraisers for NSP properties on a rotating basis.

## Action Items

- [ ] Commissioner Brown requested a copy of all back-up materials relative to budget requests.

## Topics Discussed

- FY 2010-2011 Proposed Budget
- Neighborhood Stabilization Program

## Budget & Financial Matters

- No tax increase; current tax rate remains at 78.2¢.
- Fire tax remains at 2.6¢.
- Solid Waste Household Fee remains at $102.50.
- No funding for employee salary increases.
