# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** June 21, 2010  
**Year:** 2010  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened for a special session to address several key items, including the adoption of the FY 2010-11 Budget Ordinance without any increase in property tax rates. The meeting also covered year-end financial closeouts, approval of grant applications, and interlocal agreements. The budget was detailed with specific departmental allocations, maintaining existing tax rates and fees.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted FY 2010-11 Budget Ordinance
- Authorized Year-End Closeouts
- Approved Home and Community Care Block Grant Funding Plan
- Approved BJA Justice Assistance Grant Application
- Approved Interlocal Agreement - Insurance

## Budget & Financial Matters

- No increase in property tax rate, remaining at 78.2¢
- Fire tax remains at 2.6¢
- Solid waste household fee remains at $102.50 per household
- Detailed departmental budget allocations
