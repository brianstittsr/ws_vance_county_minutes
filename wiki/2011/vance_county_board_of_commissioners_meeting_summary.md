# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** December 5, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting where they elected Terry E. Garrison as Chairman and Thomas S. Hester, Jr. as Vice-Chairman. Key appointments were made for various county positions, and reports on economic development and emergency operations were presented. The board discussed the Vance County Water Project and approved several motions to advance the project. A resolution was passed regarding the Register of Deeds' microfilm project.

## Key Decisions

- Elected Terry E. Garrison as Chairman
- Elected Thomas S. Hester, Jr. as Vice-Chairman
- Appointed Jerry L. Ayscue as County Manager
- Appointed Jonathan S. Care as County Attorney
- Approved 2012 Holiday Schedule
- Awarded bid to Atlantic Power Solutions for transfer switches
