# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Property Matter

**Date:** May 2, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss a property matter. The meeting included a closed session, after which a motion was made to advance $398,000 in Tourism Funds for land purchase for tourism purposes. The motion failed in a roll call vote. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Motion to advance $398,000 in Tourism Funds for land purchase failed.

## Action Items

- [ ] Vance County Attorney and County Manager to work with the Tourism Department on necessary documents within 30 days if the motion had passed.

## Topics Discussed

- Property matter
- Tourism Funds advancement

## Budget & Financial Matters
