# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** October 24, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss redistricting based on census data, repeal outdated ordinances, and consider grant applications for economic development. Public hearings were conducted on district boundaries and ordinance repeals. The board approved a $750,000 CDBG grant for the Semprius project and discussed a Brownfields grant application, which was tabled for further information.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the repeal of four outdated ordinances effective October 31, 2011.
- Approved a $750,000 CDBG Economic Development Grant for the Semprius project.
- Authorized performance agreements for NC Rural Economic Development Center grants totaling $550,000.
- Approved a temporary easement for DOT bridge replacement.
