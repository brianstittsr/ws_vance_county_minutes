# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - August 2011

**Date:** August 1, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss several key issues, including a public hearing on a Community Development Block Grant for Semprius, Inc., and the recognition of a community hero. The board approved the submission of a grant application for economic development and discussed the NC Catalyst Program. Committee reports on human resources and planning were also presented, with updates on the County Water Project and zoning considerations.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution to submit a grant application for Semprius, Inc. renovation project.
- Approved filling of position vacancies in the Sheriff's Office and Social Services.
- Discussed the County Water Project timeline and zoning ordinance considerations.

## Action Items

- [ ] Submit formal application to NC Department of Commerce for CDBG funding.
