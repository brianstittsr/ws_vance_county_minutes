# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on School Construction Bids

**Date:** February 21, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to award construction bids for multi-purpose rooms at three elementary schools: New Hope, L.B. Yancey, and E.M. Rollins. The meeting focused on selecting a contractor from seven bids received. The Board decided to proceed with three rooms instead of four due to budget constraints. H.G. Reynolds Co., Inc. was awarded the contract as the lowest bidder.

## Key Decisions

- Awarded construction contract to H.G. Reynolds Co., Inc. for $2,362,681.
- Decided to construct three multi-purpose rooms instead of four.
