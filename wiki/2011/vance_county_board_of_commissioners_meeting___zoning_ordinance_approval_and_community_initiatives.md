# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Zoning Ordinance Approval and Community Initiatives

**Date:** October 3, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened to discuss and approve a new zoning ordinance and map, effective November 1, 2011. Amendments were made to rezone specific parcels from R30 to Agricultural. The meeting also covered the approval of a zoning fee schedule and the scheduling of a public hearing to repeal outdated ordinances. Additionally, the Board authorized an application for a $50,000 Community Development Block Grant and recognized local community efforts.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of the proposed zoning ordinance and amended map
- Approval of the zoning fee schedule
- Scheduling of a public hearing to repeal outdated ordinances
- Authorization to apply for a $50,000 Community Development Block Grant
