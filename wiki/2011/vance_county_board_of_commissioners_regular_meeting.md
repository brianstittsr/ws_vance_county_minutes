# Vance County Board of Commissioners Regular Meeting

**Date:** November 7, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss several key issues, including a Brownfields grant application, community recognition, and public safety initiatives. The Board approved contracting with Mid-Atlantic Associates for grant assistance and recognized Mary Davis-Royster as a Community Hero. Discussions also included the establishment of a regional 911 backup center and financial support for Bearpond Volunteer Fire Department.

## Key Decisions

- Authorized contracting with Mid-Atlantic Associates for Brownfields grant application.
- Recognized Mary Davis-Royster as Community Hero for November 2011.
- Supported exploring a regional 911 backup center with Warren and Franklin Counties.
- Approved pro-rated funding for Bearpond Volunteer Fire Department.

## Action Items

- [ ] Chairman to sign proposal acceptance sheet for Brownfields grant.
- [ ] Contact Warren and Franklin Counties regarding regional 911 center.
- [ ] Develop guidelines for volunteer fire department funding requests.

## Topics Discussed

- Brownfields grant application
- Community Hero recognition
- Public Safety: 911 backup center
- Animal Control and Humane Society collaboration
- Bearpond Volunteer Fire Department funding

## Budget & Financial Matters

- EPA Brownfields Assessment grant application for $400,000.
- 911 Surcharge Funds for backup PSAP construction.
- Pro-rated $6,667 funding for Bearpond VFD.
