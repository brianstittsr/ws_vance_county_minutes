# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 12, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss various county programs and infrastructure projects. Key topics included the approval of the Rural Operating Assistance Program application, a bid for the county's wide area network, and the NC Department of Transportation Secondary Road Construction Program. Public hearings were conducted for citizen input on these matters, as well as a proposed zoning ordinance.

## Key Decisions

- Approved submission of the Rural Operating Assistance Program application
- Awarded Wide Area Network services bid to Time Warner Cable
- Approved the 2011-2012 Secondary Road Program

## Action Items

- [ ] Submission of ROAP application by Chairman, County Manager, and Finance Director
- [ ] Transition to a wide area network and new phone system

## Topics Discussed

- Rural Operating Assistance Program
- Wide Area Network bid
- Secondary Road Construction Program
- Proposed zoning ordinance

## Budget & Financial Matters

- ROAP funding allocations totaling $141,202
- Cost savings from network transition: $11,919 annually from cancelled services and $49,800 from new system

## Ordinances & Resolutions
