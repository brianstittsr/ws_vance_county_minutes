# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Water District Matters

**Date:** December 19, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met as the Water District Board to approve several key items related to the water project, including project plans, bid solicitations, and a resolution on a bond referendum. The meeting also addressed the designation of representatives for Hurricane Irene recovery efforts.

## Key Decisions

- Approved project plans and specifications subject to USDA modifications.
- Authorized staff to solicit bids for operations and maintenance services.
- Solicited construction bids according to the engineer’s timetable.
- Approved a resolution declaring the result of the bond referendum for $27,000,000 Water Bonds.
- Designated Brian Short and William Reavis to represent Vance County for Hurricane Irene recovery.
