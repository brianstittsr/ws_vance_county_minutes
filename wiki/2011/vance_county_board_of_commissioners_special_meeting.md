# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** June 21, 2011  
**Year:** 2011  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to conduct a public hearing for a CDBG grant application, adopt the FY 2011-12 Budget Ordinance, and authorize year-end closeouts. The meeting included a presentation on the CDBG-ED program, which aims to support economic development by providing funds for renovating vacant buildings and creating jobs for low to moderate-income individuals. The budget ordinance was adopted without a property tax increase, maintaining the rate at 78.2¢.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted the FY 2011-12 Budget Ordinance with no property tax increase.
- Held a public hearing for the CDBG grant application.

## Budget & Financial Matters

- No property tax increase; rate remains at 78.2¢.
- Fire tax remains at 2.6¢.
- Solid waste household fee increased to $105.00 per household per year.
