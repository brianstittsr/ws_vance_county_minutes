# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session Summary

**Date:** November 20, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss several key issues, including state budget updates, land donation, water violations, and a proposed jail inmate co-pay plan. State representatives presented a check for $4M for the county's water system. The board also considered a land donation with specific stipulations and addressed a water violation report. A proposal for a jail inmate co-pay plan was introduced to encourage responsible use of healthcare resources.

## Key Decisions

- Referred land donation matter to properties committee for further review
- Referred jail inmate co-pay plan to public safety committee for further review

## Action Items

- [ ] Properties committee to review land use options from Cooperative Extension Director
- [ ] Public safety committee to review jail inmate co-pay plan

## Topics Discussed

- State budget update and check presentation
- Land donation update
- Water violation report
- Jail inmate co-pay plan

## Budget & Financial Matters

- State budget appropriates $30 billion for various projects
- $4M for Vance County water
- $691,536 for Vance County Sheriff’s Office body cameras
- $150,000 for Vance County Economic Development
