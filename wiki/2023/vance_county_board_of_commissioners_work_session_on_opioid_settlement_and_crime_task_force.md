# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session on Opioid Settlement and Crime Task Force

**Date:** September 18, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss the utilization of opioid settlement funds and the creation of a task force to address violent crime. The meeting included presentations from the NCACC Opioid Settlement Technical Assistance Team on strategies for using the funds. An Opioid Ad-hoc Committee was formed, and discussions were held on coordinating efforts with the city's public safety committee for a Joint Violent Crime Task Force. The session concluded with a closed session on a personnel matter.

## Key Decisions

- Creation of an Opioid Ad-hoc Committee comprised of three commissioners.
- Agreement for the public safety committee to address the creation of a Joint Violent Crime Task Force.

## Action Items

- [ ] Chair Feimster appointed Commissioners Alston, Faines, and Feimster to the Opioid Task Force.

## Topics Discussed

- Opioid Settlement Funds
- Creation of Opioid Task Force
- Creation of Joint Violent Crime Task Force

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Vance County has received $447,738 in opioid settlement funds and will receive $6.2M by the end of 2039.
