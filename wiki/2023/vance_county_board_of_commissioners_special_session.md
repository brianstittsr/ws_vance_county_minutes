# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** November 1, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to administer the oath of office to the new County Manager, C. Renee Perry. Chair Yolanda J. Feimster expressed gratitude for the board's efforts in selecting the new manager and thanked Scott Elliott for his interim service. The meeting concluded with no further business discussed.

## Key Decisions

- Appointment of C. Renee Perry as Vance County Manager

## Action Items

- [ ] C. Renee Perry to assume responsibilities as County Manager

## Topics Discussed
