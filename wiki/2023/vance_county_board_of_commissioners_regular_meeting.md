# Vance County Board of Commissioners Regular Meeting

**Date:** July 10, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on July 10, 2023. Key highlights included the reappointment of Jerry Stainback to the Vance County ABC Board, recognition of achievements by EDC Director McKinley Perkinson and employee Jennifer Williams, and the adoption of an Economic Development Strategic Plan. Public hearings were conducted on economic incentives for a local company and a rezoning request for a new subdivision. The board also discussed the termination of a lease by the Green Rural Redevelopment Organization and approved contracts related to water infrastructure.

## Key Decisions

- Reappointed Jerry Stainback to the Vance County ABC Board for a three-year term.
- Adopted the Economic Development Strategic Plan.
- Authorized a $75,000 Building Reuse Grant for a local company expansion.
- Approved rezoning of a parcel at I-85 and Poplar Creek Road to Conditional Zoning R10.
- Awarded a contract to Pittsburg Tank and Tower Company for water tank restoration.
