# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Legal Matters and Budget Amendment

**Date:** February 9, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners reconvened their February 6 meeting on February 9, 2023, to address a legal matter. The meeting included a closed session to discuss legal issues, followed by the approval of Budget Amendment #29. This amendment allocated $25,000 from the General Fund Balance to cover legal fees for the Sheriff's Office. The meeting concluded without further business.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss a legal matter
- Approved Budget Amendment #29 for Sheriff's Office legal fees
