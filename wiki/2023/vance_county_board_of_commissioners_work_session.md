# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session

**Date:** October 16, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss various county projects and initiatives. Key topics included updates on the finance timeline, Phase IB Water project, detention center options, and a fire study. The board also discussed a small business initiative and appointed Renee Perry as the new county manager effective November 1, 2023.

## Key Decisions

- Appointment of Renee Perry as the new county manager effective November 1, 2023.

## Action Items

- [ ] Finance Director Katherine Bigelow to research organizations offering funding for small businesses.
- [ ] AP Triton to gather further information from each fire department directly or the county manager.

## Topics Discussed

- Finance Timeline
- Phase IB Water Update
- Detention Center Update
- Fire Study Update
- Small Business Initiative

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Discussion of the upcoming budget/finance calendar.
- NC General Assembly appropriated $4M for Phase IB Water project.
