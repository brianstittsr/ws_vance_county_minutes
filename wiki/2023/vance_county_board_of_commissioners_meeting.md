# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 5, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss various topics including public safety, employee recognition, criminal court updates, broadband grants, and property leases. Key decisions involved approving lease agreements, surplus property disposal, and grant applications. Public comments included an invitation to a safety expo and recognition of an employee of the month.

## Key Decisions

- Approved lease agreement with FVW Head Start for kitchen use at Eaton Johnson Complex.
- Declared several vehicles as surplus and authorized disposal.
- Approved inter-local agreement for JAG funds with the City of Henderson.
- Approved fireworks permit for Vance County High School.

## Action Items
