# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** June 5, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on June 5, 2023, with all commissioners present. Key highlights included the recognition of former commissioner Gordon Wilder with the Order of the Long Leaf Pine Award and the introduction of the Vance County Teen Council. Public comments were heard regarding opposition to the proposed fire restructure. The board discussed the FY 2023-2024 budget, focusing on economic development and fire service reorganization. The meeting also addressed the appointment of Scott Elliott as interim county manager and the hiring of Developmental Associates for the county manager search.

## Key Decisions

- Appointed Scott Elliott as interim county manager
- Hired Developmental Associates for county manager recruitment
- Approved FY 2022-2023 year-end budgeting and accounting transactions
