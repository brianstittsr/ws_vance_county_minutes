# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Youth Development Programs

**Date:** Not specified  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners meeting focused on various youth development programs and sponsorships. Key departments such as Recreation and Parks, Soil and Water Conservation, the Fire Department, and Cooperative Extension presented their initiatives aimed at supporting youth through educational, recreational, and preventive programs. The county also discussed its financial commitments to local schools, community colleges, and various organizations that contribute to youth and community development.

## Key Decisions

- Continued funding for Vance County Schools and Vance Granville Community College
- Provision of building lease to Gang Free, Inc. at no cost
- Annual financial support to Boys and Girls Club, Central Children’s Home, and other community organizations
