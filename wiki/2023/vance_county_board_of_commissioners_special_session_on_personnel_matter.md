# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Personnel Matter

**Date:** September 8, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session on September 8, 2023, primarily to discuss a personnel matter in a closed session. The meeting was attended by Chair Yolanda J. Feimster, Vice-Chair R. Dan Brummitt, and Commissioners Leo Kelly, Jr., and Archie B. Taylor, Jr., with Interim County Manager Scott Elliott participating via Zoom. The board unanimously approved the motion to enter into a closed session and subsequently adjourned the meeting upon returning to open session.

- Entered into closed session to discuss a personnel matter
