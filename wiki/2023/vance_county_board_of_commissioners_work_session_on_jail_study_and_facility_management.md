# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session on Jail Study and Facility Management

**Date:** August 21, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss the results of a jail study, potential sale of the Henry A. Dennis Building, fleet management improvements, and a request from Head Start to lease a kitchen. The board reviewed financial implications of various jail renovation and construction options and considered the relocation of departments from the Henry A. Dennis Building. Fleet management software was proposed to enhance operational efficiency, and a decision on leasing the Eaton Johnson kitchen will be made in upcoming meetings.

## Key Decisions

- Consideration of jail renovation or construction options
- Potential sale of the Henry A. Dennis Building
- Introduction of fleet management software

## Action Items

- [ ] Review of Head Start's request to lease the kitchen at Eaton Johnson by the properties committee on August 28 and full board on September 5
