# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** June 21, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened on June 21, 2023, to adopt the FY 2023-24 Water District Budget Ordinance and discuss an economic development project in closed session. The Board approved the budget ordinance and a resolution for the NC Building Reuse Grant related to Project Mineral, which aims to create 15 new jobs. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted the FY 2023-24 Water District Budget Ordinance
- Approved resolution for NC Building Reuse Grant for Project Mineral
