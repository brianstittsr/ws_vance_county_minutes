# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Conditional Zoning

**Date:** January 9, 2023  
**Year:** 2023  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss and approve an amendment to the Vance County Zoning Ordinance, allowing for conditional zoning. This legislative process enables more flexible zoning by allowing specific conditions for rezoning applications. A public hearing was conducted, and the ordinance was unanimously approved. The meeting also included an invocation by Rev. Joseph Ratliff.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the ordinance amending the Vance County Zoning Ordinance to allow for conditional zoning.
