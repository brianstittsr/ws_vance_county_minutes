# Vance County Board of Commissioners and Board of Education Joint Meeting

**Date:** October 21, 2019  
**Year:** 2019  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a joint session with the Board of Education to discuss several key issues, including updates on the Leandro Case, school consolidation, and the broadband initiative. The meeting also involved approving a capital funding agreement and a real estate agreement for the conveyance of Eaton Johnson school property. The session highlighted improvements in school performance and ongoing efforts to enhance broadband access in the county.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of the capital funding agreement with Vance County Schools.
- Approval of the real estate agreement for the conveyance of Eaton Johnson school property.

## Action Items

- [ ] Chairman to execute the capital funding agreement and associated documents.
- [ ] Chairman to execute the real estate agreement and associated conveyance documents.
- [ ] Board of Education to assist in promoting the broadband initiative.

## Topics Discussed

- Leandro Case Update
- School Consolidation Update
- Capital Funding Agreement
- Conveyance of Eaton Johnson School Property
- Broadband Initiative Update

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Capital funding agreement for a five-year capital improvement plan totaling $8.3 million.
- Budget amendment allocating an additional $222,250 in pay-go funds.

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Approval of the capital funding agreement
- Approval of the real estate agreement for property conveyance
