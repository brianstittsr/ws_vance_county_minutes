# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 9, 2019  
**Year:** 2019  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session where several key issues were addressed. New Finance Director Katherine Bigelow was introduced. Public comments highlighted the need for more support for the Sheriff's department. Achievements of local sports teams were recognized. The board approved a pilot program for online business listings and discussed updates on water district construction. Committee reports covered updates to emergency operations and zoning ordinances.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution for electronic listing of personal property
- Approved Change Order #2 for water district construction
- Approved Emergency Operations Plan Update
- Set public hearings for zoning and flood damage prevention ordinance amendments
- Declared certain items as surplus property
