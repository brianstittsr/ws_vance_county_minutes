# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** February 4, 2019  
**Year:** 2019  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on February 4, 2019. Key discussions included the introduction of Denita Devega as the new Director of Social Services, public comments on the proposed fire restructure, and a public hearing on economic development incentives for Southeastern Specialty Vehicles. The board also received the FY 2017-18 Audit Report and updates on the Stepping Up Initiative and Henderson-Oxford Airport.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of resolution supporting job creation and economic development for Southeastern Specialty Vehicles
- Receipt of the FY 2017-18 Audit Report

## Action Items

- [ ] Chairman authorized to execute documents related to the Building Reuse Grant

## Topics Discussed

- Introduction of new Director of Social Services
- Proposed fire restructure
- Economic development incentives
- FY 2017-18 Audit Report
- Stepping Up Initiative
- Henderson-Oxford Airport update

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Discussion of $2.6 million budget for county fire services
- Approval of $130,000 Building Reuse Grant with $6,500 local match

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Resolution in support of job creation and economic development

## Attendees

- Chairman Archie B. Taylor, Jr.
- Vice-Chairman Gordon Wilder
- Commissioners Dan Brummitt, Carolyn Faines, Yolanda J. Feimster, Thomas S. Hester, Jr., Leo Kelly, Jr.
- County Manager Jordan McMillen
- Finance Director David C. Beck
- County Attorney Jonathan S. Care
- Clerk to the Board Kelly H. Grissom

## Public Comments

- Krista Duncan supported the fire restructure proposal, questioning the funding disparity between VCFD and volunteer departments.
- Phyllis Stainback opposed the fire restructure, emphasizing the importance of response time and manpower.
- Deborah Brown supported the fire restructure and suggested forming Vance County Fire and Rescue.
