# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** June 17, 2019  
**Year:** 2019  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to adopt the FY 2019-20 Budget Ordinance, approve the Salary Progression Policy, and convene the Water District Board for the Water District Budget Ordinance. The total general fund budget was set at $48,537,425, with significant allocations for public safety, rural fire protection, and school capital investments. The budget also addressed broadband needs and employee retention through a salary progression plan.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted the FY 2019-20 Budget Ordinance
- Approved the Salary Progression Policy
- Adopted the FY 2019-20 Water District Budget Ordinance

- Fire tax increase to 8.9 cents per $100
- School Capital investment: over $5.2 million
