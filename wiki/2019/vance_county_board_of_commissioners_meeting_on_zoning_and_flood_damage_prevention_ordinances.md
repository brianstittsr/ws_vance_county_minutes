# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Zoning and Flood Damage Prevention Ordinances

**Date:** October 7, 2019  
**Year:** 2019  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss amendments to the Zoning Ordinance and the Flood Damage Prevention Ordinance. The meeting included public hearings for both topics, though no public comments were made. The board unanimously approved amendments to regulate Land Clearing Inert Debris Landfills and updated the Flood Damage Prevention Ordinance in compliance with FEMA requirements.

## Key Decisions

- Approved amendments to the Vance County Zoning Ordinance regarding Land Clearing Inert Debris Landfills.
- Approved amendments to the Vance County Flood Damage Prevention Ordinance.
