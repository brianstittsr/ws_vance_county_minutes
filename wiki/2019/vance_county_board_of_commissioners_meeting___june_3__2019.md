# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - June 3, 2019

**Date:** 2019-06-03  
**Year:** 2019  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session to discuss various matters including public comments on fire tax increases, budget allocations for economic development, and the FY 2019-20 Proposed Budget. Key decisions included approving contracts for renovations and revising fire department contract language. Public concerns focused on equitable funding for fire departments and opposition to fire tax increases.

## Key Decisions

- Approved contract with Oakley, Collier Architects for DSS and Senior Center design at Eaton Johnson.
- Revised contract language for volunteer fire departments to waive audit requirements.
- Declared certain vehicles as surplus property.

## Action Items

- [ ] County staff to perform FY 2018-19 year-end budgeting and accounting transactions.
- [ ] Finance Director to dispose of surplus property.

## Topics Discussed

- Fire tax increase opposition
- Economic development budget allocation

## Budget & Financial Matters
