# Vance County Board of Commissioners Emergency Meeting on Senate Bill 214

**Date:** April 24, 2026  
**Year:** 2026  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held an emergency meeting to discuss and vote on a resolution opposing Senate Bill 214, Part V, Section 5. The bill is currently pending before the North Carolina General Assembly. The commissioners unanimously approved the resolution against the bill. The meeting was brief and concluded shortly after the resolution was passed.

## Key Decisions

- Adoption of a resolution in opposition to Senate Bill 214, Part V, Section 5.
