# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session

**Date:** March 17, 2026  
**Year:** 2026  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss several key issues, including the solid waste fee waiver policy, a proposed AI policy, the potential establishment of a separate Board of Equalization and Review, and health insurance premium adjustments. The Board decided to maintain the current solid waste fee collection practice and explore creating a separate ENR board. Discussions on the AI policy and health insurance premiums will continue at the next meeting.

## Key Decisions

- Maintain current practice of collecting solid waste fees without a waiver policy.
- Explore creation of a separate Board of Equalization and Review.

## Action Items

- [ ] AI policy to be reviewed and considered for approval at the April 6 meeting.
- [ ] Ms. Perry to return with a proposed resolution for a separate ENR board.
