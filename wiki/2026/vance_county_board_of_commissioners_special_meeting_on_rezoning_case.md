# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Rezoning Case

**Date:** April 20, 2026  
**Year:** 2026  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to deliberate and take action on Rezoning Case #ZMA26-001, concerning a property off NC Highway 158 Business. The meeting followed a public hearing held earlier in the month. The Board emphasized maintaining order and respect during the proceedings. Ultimately, the rezoning amendment was approved with a 6-1 vote.
