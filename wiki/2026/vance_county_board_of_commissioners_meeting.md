# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** March 2, 2026  
**Year:** 2026  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened to discuss various county matters, including employee recognition, public comments on taxes, and several administrative and financial policies. Key decisions included the approval of a penny shortage policy, the selection of an architectural firm for a new EMS substation, and changes to employee benefits. Public comments focused on tax issues, and the meeting concluded with the approval of the consent agenda.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the Vance County Penny Shortage Policy.
- Authorized negotiations with Oakley Collier Architects for EMS Substation project.
- Awarded service weapon and badge to retired Sergeant Clevon Dozier.
- Tabled discussion on employee health insurance premium changes.
- Approved vacation accrual policy adjustment effective July 1, 2026.
- Approved birthday leave policy effective July 1, 2026.
