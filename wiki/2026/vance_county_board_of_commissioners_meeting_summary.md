# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Year:** 2026  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting on February 16, 2026. Key highlights included recognizing the employee of the month, approving untimely tax exemption applications, and receiving updates from the Fire Department and Finance Director. The board also approved a resolution designating official bank depositories and discussed updates to the EMS fee schedule and Medical Director contract. A new dress and appearance policy for county employees was adopted, and a resolution was passed to thank responders to Winter Storm Fern.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of untimely tax exemption applications
- Approval of resolution designating official bank depositories
- Approval of new EMS fee for special events
- Renewal of Medical Director contract for Dr. Richard Benson
