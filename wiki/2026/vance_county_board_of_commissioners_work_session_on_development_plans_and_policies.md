# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session on Development Plans and Policies

**Date:** April 20, 2026  
**Year:** 2026  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss the draft Unified Development Ordinance (UDO) and the proposed update to the Comprehensive Development Plan. The UDO aims to consolidate and modernize land use regulations, while the Comprehensive Plan update seeks to align future growth with current community needs. Additionally, new policies regarding public hearings and public comments were introduced for review.

## Key Decisions

- Approval to review the draft Unified Development Ordinance
- Initiation of the Comprehensive Development Plan update
