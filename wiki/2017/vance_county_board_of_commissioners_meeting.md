# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 5, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss various community concerns, including the status of the Human Relations Commission, vegetation issues on NC Hwy 39, and drug-related problems in the community. The board also reviewed and approved several untimely exemption applications. Additionally, the board discussed offers to purchase county-owned properties and authorized the upset bid process for their sale.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of untimely exemption applications
- Authorization of upset bid process for two county-owned properties

## Action Items

- [ ] Revamp the Human Relations Commission
- [ ] Communicate with the Department of Transportation and Army Corps of Engineers about vegetation on Hwy 39

## Topics Discussed

- Human Relations Commission
- Vegetation on NC Hwy 39
- Drug issues in the community
- Exemption applications
- County property sales

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Resolution authorizing upset bid process for sale of real property at US Hwy 1 South
- Resolution authorizing upset bid process for sale of real property at Squirrel Hollow
