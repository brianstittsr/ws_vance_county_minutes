# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** June 26, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners reconvened its June 22, 2017 meeting in a special session on June 26, 2017. The meeting focused on the approval of the FY 2017-18 budgets for both the Vance County Water District and Vance County. The Water District budget was set at $1,157,971, with a significant reduction in the general fund contribution. The Vance County budget emphasized education, public safety, and human services, maintaining no tax increases. Both budgets were unanimously approved.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of FY 2017-18 Water District Budget Ordinance
- Approval of FY 2017-18 Vance County Budget Ordinance
