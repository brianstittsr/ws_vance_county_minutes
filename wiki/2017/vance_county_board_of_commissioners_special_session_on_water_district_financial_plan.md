# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Water District Financial Plan

**Date:** May 25, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened a special session to discuss and approve the Vance County Water District Multi-Year Financial Plan. The meeting focused on ensuring the financial sustainability of the county's water system, which has grown from 620 to 1,229 customers since FY 2015. The plan aims to further increase the customer base through incentives over the next five to seven years. The board unanimously approved the plan.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of the Vance County Water District Multi-Year Financial Plan

## Action Items

- [ ] Implement incentives to increase the water system's customer base over five to seven years
