# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Legal Matters and Equipment Sale

**Date:** February 20, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened for a regular session to discuss a legal matter. The meeting included a closed session, after which the board approved a motion to allow Onyx Asset Advisors to sell equipment on which the county holds a lien. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss a legal matter.
- Approved the sale of five pieces of equipment by Onyx Asset Advisors, with authorization for the Chairman to execute necessary agreements.

## Action Items

- [ ] Chairman to execute agreements related to the equipment sale.

## Topics Discussed

- Legal matter discussion
- Sale of equipment with a lien
