# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Economic Development

**Date:** November 28, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss economic development incentives for a new company. The meeting included closed sessions and public hearings on proposed grants and incentive agreements. The board approved resolutions for a Building Reuse Grant and an Economic Incentive Grant Agreement with Mako Medical Laboratories, which will create 126 jobs. A budget amendment was also approved to allocate funds for Project Mikey.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution authorizing a Building Reuse Grant of $500,000.
- Approved a local incentive agreement not to exceed $425,000.
- Approved a budget amendment of $250,000 for Project Mikey.

## Action Items
