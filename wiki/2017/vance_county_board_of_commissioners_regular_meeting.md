# Vance County Board of Commissioners Regular Meeting

**Date:** February 6, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss several key issues, including the Tar River Regional Hazard Mitigation Plan, FY 2017-18 Budget, and a rezoning request. The board also addressed the Water District Phase 3 Capital Project and property matters. Public hearings were conducted, but no public comments were made. Key decisions included the approval of a rezoning request and authorization of the Water District Phase 3 Capital Project Ordinance.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of rezoning request for 4422 Warrenton Road
- Adoption of Water District Phase 3 Capital Project Ordinance
- Authorization of upset bid process for 517 Neathery Street
