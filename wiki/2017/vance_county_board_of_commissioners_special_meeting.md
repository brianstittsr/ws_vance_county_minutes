# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** September 12, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss a legal and a personnel matter. The meeting took place at the Vance County Administration Building with most commissioners present, except Commissioner Yolanda J. Feimster. The board entered a closed session for the discussions and returned to open session with no further business to address.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss legal and personnel matters

## Topics Discussed

- Legal matter
- Personnel matter
