# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Golden LEAF Grant and Equipment Lease

**Date:** December 11, 2017  
**Year:** 2017  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss and approve the Golden LEAF grant agreement and an equipment lease for Mako Medical Laboratories. The grant, amounting to $522,987, requires Mako Medical to meet specific job creation and salary benchmarks. The meeting also included the approval of Budget Amendment #15 to accommodate the grant funds for equipment purchase and lease.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the Golden LEAF grant agreement for Mako Medical Laboratories.
- Approved Budget Amendment #15 to incorporate grant funds.
- Approved the equipment lease with Mako Medical Laboratories.
