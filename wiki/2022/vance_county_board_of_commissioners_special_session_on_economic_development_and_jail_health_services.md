# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Economic Development and Jail Health Services

**Date:** October 24, 2022  
**Year:** 2022  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss an economic development project in a closed session and address urgent jail health service needs in an open session. Southern Health Partners terminated its contract, leading to a temporary agreement with StarMed Healthcare. The board approved retroactive contracts and a budget amendment to cover interim costs, despite concerns over high expenses.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss an economic development project.
- Approved minutes of the October 3, 2022 closed session.
- Retroactively approved first and second week contracts with StarMed Healthcare.
- Authorized the County Manager to finalize contracts for up to 60 additional days.
- Approved Budget Amendment #21 from Fund Balance for jail health services.

## Action Items

- [ ] County Manager to finalize contracts with StarMed Healthcare for up to 60 additional days.
- [ ] Staff to continue searching for a long-term solution for jail health services.

## Topics Discussed

- Economic development project
- Jail health services

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Approved a $400,000 budget amendment from the General Fund Balance for interim jail health costs.
