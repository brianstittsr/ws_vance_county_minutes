# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Property Sale and Loan Agreement

**Date:** September 19, 2022  
**Year:** 2022  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to finalize the sale of property at the Henderson-Vance Industrial Park and to approve a loan agreement for the construction of a shell building. The board approved the sale of 7.15 acres to Henderson-Vance Industrial Park, Inc. for $185,900 and authorized a $2.9 million loan for the project's development. The meeting concluded with the adoption of related budget amendments and ordinances.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the sale of 7.15 acres to Henderson-Vance Industrial Park, Inc. for $185,900.
- Approved a $2.9 million loan agreement for the construction of a 50,000 sq. ft. shell building.
- Adopted the HVIP Loan Project Ordinance and related budget amendments.
