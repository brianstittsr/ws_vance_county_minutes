# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 6, 2022  
**Year:** 2022  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session where public comments were heard regarding opioid treatment and a mixed-use residential development. The board discussed the abandonment of a portion of Cardinal Drive and recognized an employee of the month. Updates were provided on solid waste management and economic development, including a marketing campaign and a strategic plan. The Water District Board addressed issues related to water system management and grant applications. Committee reports included funding requests for the rescue squad.

## Key Decisions

- Tabled decision on Cardinal Drive abandonment
- Approved conversion of water tap from wet to dry for Lot 30 Dabney Woods Subdivision
- Authorized grant application for water system asset assessment
- Approved engineering services amendment for Phase 1B water system expansion
