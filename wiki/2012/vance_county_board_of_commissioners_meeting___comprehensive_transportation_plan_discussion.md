# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Comprehensive Transportation Plan Discussion

**Date:** August 6, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss the Comprehensive Transportation Plan, which includes future transportation improvements. The plan aims to address future transportation deficiencies and guide decisions. The Board approved the transportation maps with exceptions for Dabney Drive and Corbitt Road. Additionally, bids for the 2010 Scattered Site Program were awarded, and a resolution of appreciation for Mary Helen Jones was adopted.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the Vance County Comprehensive Transportation Maps with exceptions for Dabney Drive and Corbitt Road.
- Awarded bids for the 2010 Scattered Site Program for rehabilitation or urgent repair.
- Adopted a resolution of appreciation for Mary Helen Jones.

## Action Items

- [ ] Further study requested for Dabney Drive and Corbitt Road traffic movement and pedestrian safety.
- [ ] Re-bid required for lead abatement due to insufficient bids.
