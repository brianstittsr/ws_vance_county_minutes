# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Property Appeals and Mental Health Authority Resolution

**Date:** May 7, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to address property value appeals and discuss the dissolution of the Five County Mental Health Authority. Property appeals were reviewed for several properties, with the board deciding to maintain current valuations based on the 2008 Schedule of Values. Additionally, a resolution was passed to dissolve the Five County Mental Health Authority and establish a new area authority for a fifteen-county catchment area, aligning with state mandates.

## Key Decisions

- Maintained current property values for all appealed properties.
- Approved the dissolution of Five County Mental Health Authority and establishment of a new area authority.
