# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Water Sales Agreement

**Date:** July 23, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to discuss and approve a Water Sales Agreement with the City of Henderson for Phases 2A and 2B of a water project. The meeting included a closed session to discuss attorney-client privileged matters. Upon return to open session, the agreement was approved with minor typographical corrections. Concerns were raised about fees and rates, but the agreement was deemed necessary to secure $4.5 million in grants and loans.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of the Water Sales Agreement between Vance County and the City of Henderson for Phases 2A and 2B.

## Action Items

- [ ] County Manager Jerry L. Ayscue to work with the engineer to assess the impact of reservation and tap fees on customer rates.

## Topics Discussed

- Water Sales Agreement
- Attorney-client privileged matters
- Water project Phases 2A and 2B

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Discussion of $4.5 million in grants and loans at risk if the agreement is delayed.
