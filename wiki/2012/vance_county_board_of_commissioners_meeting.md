# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 10, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to address various community and administrative issues. Key topics included the demolition and reconstruction of a deteriorated home, a rezoning request for Morris Wilson Road, and enforcement actions for a dilapidated property. Financial matters such as the disposition of records and lease purchase agreements for 911 equipment were also discussed. Public comments raised concerns about zoning ordinances related to recreational vehicles.

## Key Decisions

- Approved revision for 1140 Stagecoach Road from rehabilitation to demolition and reconstruction.
- Approved disposition of financial, payroll, and ambulance records.
- Approved rezoning request for Morris Wilson Road.
- Authorized legal action for property compliance at 2265 St. Matthews Street.
- Approved lease purchase of console radio equipment for the 911 back-up center.
