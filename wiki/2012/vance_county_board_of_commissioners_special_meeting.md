# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** May 24, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to receive the FY 2012-2013 Proposed Budget, schedule the budget hearing and work sessions, and approve a resolution of appreciation for Kay Fields. The proposed budget maintains current expenditure levels with no increase in ad valorem or fire taxes and a reduced reliance on fund balance appropriations. The Board also scheduled the budget hearing and work sessions and passed a resolution honoring Kay Fields for her service.

## Key Decisions

- Received the FY 2012-2013 Proposed Budget
- Scheduled the Budget Hearing for June 4, 2012
- Approved a Resolution of Appreciation for Kay Fields

## Action Items

## Topics Discussed

## Budget & Financial Matters

- General Fund budget is a 'hold the line' budget
- No increase in ad valorem or fire taxes
- Solid Waste Household Fee remains at $105.00
- Reduced reliance on fund balance appropriations
- No funding for employee salary increases
