# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Hazard Mitigation Plan and 911 Center Discussion

**Date:** February 6, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss amendments to the County’s Hazard Mitigation Plan and the development of a back-up 911 center. The meeting included a public hearing on the Hazard Mitigation Plan, where updates were suggested to include current census data and zoning verbiage. A proclamation was approved to recognize the 100th anniversary of Girl Scouting. Discussions on the 911 center focused on site selection and funding constraints. The FY 2010-11 Audit Report was presented, highlighting a decrease in the fund balance and potential future financial impacts.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of proclamation recognizing Girl Scout Week.
- Authorization to proceed with the 911 back-up center using available funds.

## Action Items

- [ ] Update Hazard Mitigation Plan with 2010 census data and zoning verbiage.
