# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Water Sales Agreement

**Date:** July 23, 2012  
**Year:** 2012  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss and approve a Water Sales Agreement with the City of Henderson for Phases 2A and 2B of a water project. The meeting included a closed session for attorney-client privileged matters. The agreement aims to provide potable water to citizens and prevent the loss of $4.5 million in grants and loans. Concerns were raised about fees and rates, but the agreement was approved with minor typographical corrections.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of the Water Sales Agreement between Vance County and the City of Henderson for Phases 2A and 2B.

## Action Items

- [ ] County Manager Jerry L. Ayscue to work with the engineer to assess the impact of reservation and tap fees on customer rates.

## Topics Discussed

- Water Sales Agreement
- Attorney-client privileged matters
- Water project Phases 2A and 2B

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Potential impact of reservation and tap fees on customer rates
- Risk of losing $4.5 million in grants and loans

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Approval of Water Sales Agreement with typographical corrections
