# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting on Personnel Matters

**Date:** August 30, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session on August 30, 2016, at the Commissioners’ Conference Room in Henderson, NC. The meeting's primary purpose was to enter into a closed session to discuss personnel matters. All commissioners were present, along with the County Manager and County Attorney. The meeting concluded with no further business discussed in the open session.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss personnel matters

## Topics Discussed

- Personnel matters
