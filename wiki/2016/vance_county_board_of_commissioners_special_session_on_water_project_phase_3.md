# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Water Project Phase 3

**Date:** December 29, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss Phase 3 of the county water project. The meeting focused on receiving a Letter of Conditions from USDA, which includes a $1,000,000 grant and a $212,000 loan. The Water Committee reviewed the financial analysis and recommended proceeding with the project, which is expected to have a positive financial impact. The board approved the resolution to accept the USDA conditions and authorized LKC Engineering to begin the design phase.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution accepting USDA conditions for Phase 3 of the water project.
- Authorized LKC Engineering to proceed with the design phase.

## Budget & Financial Matters

- $1,000,000 grant and $212,000 loan from USDA

- Estimated positive impact of $16,500 per year
