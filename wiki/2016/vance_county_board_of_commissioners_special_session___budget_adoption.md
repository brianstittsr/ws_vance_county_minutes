# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session - Budget Adoption

**Date:** June 9, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to adopt the FY 2016-17 Budget and discuss a personnel matter in closed session. The meeting included discussions on additional funding for the Granville-Vance Health Department. The budget was approved unanimously with one abstention.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted the FY 2016-17 Budget.
- Approved a one-time additional funding of $150,000 to the Granville-Vance Health Department.
