# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Property Valuation Appeals

**Date:** June 9, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to review property valuation appeals as the Board of Equalization and Review. Several property owners contested the assessed values of their properties, citing discrepancies and requesting adjustments. The board listened to each case, discussed potential adjustments, and requested additional information where necessary. The meeting concluded with plans for further review and adjustments based on the discussions.

## Key Decisions

- Ralph Ellington's property to be reviewed for Farm Use classification.
- Darnell and Carla Carter-Rose's property value to be re-evaluated.
- Glenn Nunnery's mobile home to be listed as personal property.
- Richard St. Pierre's parcels to be considered for combination and value comparison with neighboring property.
- James Crawford's property value to be adjusted to $144,000.
- Dennis Massey to provide comps for further review of his property's value.

## Action Items

- [ ] Ms. Brooks to investigate Farm Use classification for Ellington's property.
- [ ] Appraiser to re-evaluate Carter-Rose's property and neighboring parcel.
- [ ] Change Nunnery's mobile home listing to personal property.
- [ ] Compare St. Pierre's property value with neighbor's and consider parcel combination.
- [ ] Adjust Crawford's property value to $144,000 and review area properties.
