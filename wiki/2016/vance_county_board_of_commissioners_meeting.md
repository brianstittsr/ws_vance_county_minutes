# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 6, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session to discuss various economic development initiatives, public safety, and administrative matters. Key decisions included approving grants for local businesses, discussing cemetery maintenance funding, and considering the display of the national motto on county buildings. The meeting also addressed water district issues and personnel reorganization within the planning and development department.

## Key Decisions

- Approved a $150,000 NC Department of Commerce Building Reuse Grant for Ruby Jean Investments, LLC.
- Authorized a $412,960 economic development grant to Mars Petcare.
- Approved the consulting agreement with the MAPS Group for a salary and benefits study.
- Tabled the decision on displaying 'In God We Trust' on county buildings.

## Action Items

- [ ] Mr. Faucette to submit a formal budget request for cemetery maintenance funding.
- [ ] Staff to research collections agency options for delinquent water accounts.
- [ ] Properties Committee to review the 'In God We Trust' display proposal.
