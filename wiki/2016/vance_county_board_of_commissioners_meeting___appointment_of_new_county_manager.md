# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - Appointment of New County Manager

**Date:** August 19, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners reconvened their regular meeting on August 19, 2016. The primary focus was the swearing-in of Jordan McMillen as the new Vance County Manager. Chairman Gordon Wilder expressed gratitude to Robert Murphy for serving as interim county manager and thanked the staff for their efforts during the transition. The meeting concluded with no further business discussed.

## Key Decisions

- Appointment of Jordan McMillen as Vance County Manager

## Action Items

- [ ] Jordan McMillen to assume duties as County Manager

## Topics Discussed

- Appointment of new County Manager
