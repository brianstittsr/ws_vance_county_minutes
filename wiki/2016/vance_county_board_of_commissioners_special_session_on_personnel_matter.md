# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Personnel Matter

**Date:** July 5, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners reconvened their June 9, 2016 meeting in a special session on July 5, 2016. The meeting was held to discuss a personnel matter in a closed session. All commissioners were present, along with key county staff. The session was brief, and no further business was conducted upon returning to open session.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss a personnel matter

## Topics Discussed

- Personnel matter
