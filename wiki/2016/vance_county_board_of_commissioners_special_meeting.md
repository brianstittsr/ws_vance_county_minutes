# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** May 17, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners reconvened a special meeting initially held on May 12, 2016. The meeting took place on May 17, 2016, at the Vance County Administration Building. All commissioners were present, along with the Interim County Manager and Human Resources Director. The primary agenda was a closed session to discuss a personnel matter. The meeting was adjourned with no further business discussed.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss a personnel matter

## Topics Discussed
