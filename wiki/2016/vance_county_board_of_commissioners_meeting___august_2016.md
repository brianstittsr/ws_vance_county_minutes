# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting - August 2016

**Date:** August 1, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to address property valuation appeals, hear updates from Vance Granville Community College, and manage various administrative matters. Key decisions included adjustments to several property valuations and the authorization of the upset bid process for a property on Twelve Oaks Lane. The board also expressed appreciation for Interim County Manager Robert Murphy's service and addressed public concerns regarding water billing.

## Key Decisions

- Adjusted property valuations for several parcels
- Retained property valuations for other parcels
- Authorized upset bid process for property at 436 Twelve Oaks Lane
- Declared office furniture as surplus
