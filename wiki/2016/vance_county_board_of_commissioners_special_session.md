# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** May 12, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss a personnel matter in closed session. Upon returning to open session, the board addressed a Neighborhood Stabilization Program issue, specifically the need for county funds to cover closing costs for a property. The meeting concluded with a decision to reconvene on May 17, 2016.

## Key Decisions

- Entered into closed session to discuss a personnel matter.
- Authorized up to $4,000 for closing costs for 661 Charles Street under the Neighborhood Stabilization Program.

## Action Items

- [ ] Authorize up to $4,000 for closing costs for 661 Charles Street - Responsible: County Attorney Jonathan S. Care
