# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** July 11, 2016  
**Year:** 2016  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss various agenda items including appointments to the ABC Board, public comments on financial policy and water system issues, and updates on community projects such as the 'Walk the Beat' fitness program. Key decisions included reappointments and new appointments to the ABC Board, adoption of financial policy guidelines, and approval of contracts related to the animal shelter and building maintenance.

## Key Decisions

- Reappointed Andrea Harris to the ABC Board for a three-year term.
- Appointed Anne Tucker to the ABC Board for a three-year term.
- Appointed Nancy Wilson as Chair of the ABC Board for a one-year term.
- Adopted financial policy guidelines.
- Authorized use of grounds for an outdoor fitness area.
- Approved Change Order #3 for animal shelter construction.
- Awarded fencing contract to Seward Fencing for the animal shelter.
- Extended Henderson Collegiate lease until June 30, 2018.
- Awarded contracts for roof and masonry restoration of the Dennis Building.
