# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Fire Department and Code Enforcement

**Date:** December 16, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address issues related to the fire department and code enforcement. The board approved the release of frozen funds and positions in the fire department budget following the hiring of a Fire Marshal and interim fire chief. Concerns were raised about the Henderson City Council's decision to end a long-standing agreement with the county for code enforcement services. Additionally, Chair Sean A. Alston, Sr. announced his resignation from two boards, leading to appointments of new representatives.

## Key Decisions

- Release of frozen funds and positions in the fire department budget.
- Appointment of Thomas S. Hester, Jr. to the Henderson-Vance Economic Development Commission.
- Appointment of R. Dan Brummitt to the Henderson-Vance Industrial Park Board.
