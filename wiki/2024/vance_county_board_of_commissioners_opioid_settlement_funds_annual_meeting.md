# Vance County Board of Commissioners Opioid Settlement Funds Annual Meeting

**Date:** September 16, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held their annual meeting to discuss the allocation of opioid settlement funds. The meeting was attended by Chair R. Dan Brummitt, Vice-Chair Leo Kelly, Jr., and Commissioner Archie B. Taylor, Jr., with several commissioners absent. County Manager C. Renee Perry reported that Vance County has received $1,861,404 in opioid settlement funds and will receive a total of $6.2 million by 2038. The county has chosen Option A from the memorandum of understanding to fund high-impact strategies to address the opioid epidemic. Discussions included the need for a more diverse opioid committee.

## Key Decisions

- Selection of Option A for funding strategies
- Discussion on diversifying the opioid committee
