# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 3, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session on September 3, 2024. Key topics included public opposition to property tax increases and proposed developments, acceptance of grant funding for water infrastructure, and the issuance of a revenue bond anticipation note for water system improvements. The board approved an amended resolution for a grant from the 2023 Appropriations Act and discussed financing for the Kittrell Township water project.

## Key Decisions

- Approved amended resolution accepting $3,940,000 grant for Phase 1B water project.
- Authorized issuance of a $1,672,000 Water System Revenue Bond Anticipation Note.
