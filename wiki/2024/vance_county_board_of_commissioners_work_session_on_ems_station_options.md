# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session on EMS Station Options

**Date:** March 18, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to discuss potential land options for an EMS main station and substation. The meeting was attended by most commissioners and key county staff. The session was conducted in a closed format, and no formal decisions were made during this meeting.

## Topics Discussed

- Possible land options for EMS main station and substation

- Vice-Chair Leo Kelly, Jr.

- Commissioner Carolyn Faines

- Commissioner Archie B. Taylor, Jr.
- County Manager C. Renee Perry
