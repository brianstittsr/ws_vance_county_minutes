# Vance County Board of Equalization and Review Meeting

**Date:** December 9, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Equalization and Review held a special session to address appeals from the 2024 tax revaluation. Several property owners presented their cases regarding property value assessments they believed were inaccurate. Decisions were made to uphold or adjust property values based on evidence presented. Some appeals were continued to a later date for further review.

## Key Decisions

- Upheld property values for William Ashley Fowler, Jr., Neil & Patricia Gillespie, and Kinita Guzman.
- Lowered the value of Robert T. Breedlove, Jr.'s Montgomery Street Lot to $1,210.
- Continued appeals for Christopher Lawson and Steven Wade Harp to December 16, 2024.
