# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Budget Approval

**Date:** June 24, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to approve the FY 2024-25 Budget Ordinance and the audit contract for FY 2023-24. The budget was approved with a 16% increase over the previous year, despite concerns over a 10 cent tax increase and a 23% salary increase for the board. The budget includes significant allocations for county improvements, including a new EMS station and jail.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of FY 2024-25 Budget Ordinance
- Approval of audit contract for FY 2023-24
