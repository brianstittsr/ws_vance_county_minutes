# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** June 3, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session on June 3, 2024. The meeting included a special presentation honoring Senior Master Sergeant George Kevlin Davis on his upcoming 100th birthday. Public comments addressed concerns over property tax values. The Employee of the Year was recognized, and the FY 2022-23 Audit Report was presented, highlighting issues with the former finance officer. The Board of Equalization and Review heard property value disputes, and public hearings on the FY 2024-25 budget were held. Budget amendments for year-end closeout were approved.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of FY 2022-23 Audit Report
- Continuation of property value disputes to July 1 meeting
- Authorization for FY 2023-24 year-end budgeting and accounting transactions
