# Vance County Board of Commissioners Work Session on FY 2024-25 Budget

**Date:** June 13, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a work session to review and discuss the proposed FY 2024-25 budget. Key topics included funding for rural fire departments, capital projects such as EMS stations and jail renovations, and a proposed tax increase. The board also addressed funding for the KARTS Around Town Shuttle and a new financial position in the Sheriff’s Office. A special meeting was scheduled to adopt the budget.

## Key Decisions

- Set part-time pay for rural fire departments at $17.00 per hour
- Fire tax rate set at 7 cents
- Disburse an additional $36,000 to each fire department
- Annual allocation to each fire department set at $130,000
- Proposed tax rate set at 71.3 cents
- Allocate $37,500 for KARTS Around Town Shuttle for six months
- Include salary for a financial position in the Sheriff’s Office
