# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Tax Appeals

**Date:** October 14, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to review appeals related to the 2024 tax revaluation. Several property owners contested the assessed values of their properties, citing discrepancies with market values and property conditions. The board upheld most of the current valuations, with a few appeals continued for further review. The meeting concluded with the approval of the in-house tax revaluation appeal decisions.

## Key Decisions

- Upheld the tax value for Pamela Session's property at $197,904.
- Continued Crystal Ross's appeal for further review.
- Upheld the tax value for Michael D. Jones's property at $106,197.
- Continued Murile Udeshi's appeal for further review.
- Upheld the tax value for Cathy & Frank Robertson's property at $135,452.
- Upheld values for Anthony D. White, Paul & Cynthia Jordan, and Linda & Chad Stevenson.
