# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Tax Revaluation Appeals

**Date:** September 25, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners convened to address appeals related to the 2024 tax revaluation. Several property owners contested the assessed values of their properties, citing discrepancies with fair market values. The board upheld most values due to lack of evidence from appellants but continued some cases to allow for further evidence gathering. A property ownership issue was referred back to the tax office for correction.

## Key Decisions

- Upheld tax values for properties where appellants did not show or provide sufficient evidence.
- Continued appeals for Juanita Fogg, H. A. Johnson, Jr., Tommy Pernell, and John & Charles Allen to allow time for additional information.
- Referred Paul Ross's parcel issue to the tax office for correction.
