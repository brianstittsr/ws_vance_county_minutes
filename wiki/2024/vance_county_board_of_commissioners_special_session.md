# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** February 15, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to appoint an interim finance officer and discuss an economic development matter. The meeting included the approval of minutes from previous closed sessions and the appointment of Charles Murray, Jr. as the interim finance officer. The board also entered a closed session to deliberate on an economic development issue.

## Key Decisions

- Approval of minutes from December 4, 2023, December 20, 2023, and January 8, 2024 closed sessions.
- Appointment of Charles Murray, Jr. as interim finance officer effective February 19, 2024.

## Action Items

- [ ] Charles Murray, Jr. to assume role of interim finance officer starting February 19, 2024.

## Topics Discussed

- Appointment of finance officer
- Economic development matter

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Discussion on interim finance officer to assist through budget process
