# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on FY 2024-25 Budget

**Date:** May 28, 2024  
**Year:** 2024  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to receive the FY 2024-25 proposed budget, schedule a budget hearing, and plan budget work sessions. The proposed budget maintains current service levels while addressing priorities such as competitive pay and community health. Key highlights include a decrease in property and fire tax rates, increased sales tax revenue, and investments in education and health. The board unanimously approved the receipt of the proposed budget.

## Key Decisions

- Received the FY 2024-25 proposed budget
- Scheduled budget hearing for June 3, 2024
- Scheduled budget work sessions for June 10 and June 13, 2024

## Topics Discussed

- FY 2024-25 Proposed Budget
- Tax rate adjustments
- Budget hearing and work sessions

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Decrease in general fund property tax rate from .89¢ to 63.3¢
- Decrease in fire tax rate from 8.9¢ to 5.94¢
- Appropriation of $2,310,300 from general fund balance
- Projected sales tax revenue increase
- Cost-of-living adjustment for employees
- Increased funding for Granville-Vance Health Department, Vance County Schools, and Vance-Granville Community College
- Increase in solid waste household fee from $120 to $125
