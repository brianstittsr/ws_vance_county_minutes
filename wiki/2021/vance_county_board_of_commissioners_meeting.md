# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 7, 2021  
**Year:** 2021  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session to discuss various county matters. Key topics included employee recognition, EMS updates, water district operations, property committee reports, and financial resolutions for school improvements. The board approved several motions related to property management and financial agreements.

## Key Decisions

- Approved change order for Eaton Johnson project for $26,398.85.
- Authorized foreclosure process for Henderson Laundry property with conditions.
- Approved lease agreement with Green Rural Redevelopment Organization for Eaton Johnson kitchen.
- Authorized drafting of lease agreement for Eaton Johnson wing with FVW Opportunity Head Start.
- Approved resolution for installment financing agreement for school improvements.

## Action Items

- [ ] County Manager to check availability of state funds for water district assistance.
- [ ] Staff to reinitiate foreclosure process for Henderson Laundry property.
- [ ] Staff to draft lease agreement for Eaton Johnson wing and contract with architect.
