# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** October 4, 2021  
**Year:** 2021  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners met to discuss various county matters, including public comments on the Sheriff's Office, employee recognition, rezoning requests, and several committee reports. Key decisions included rezoning approvals, HVAC replacements, and participation in the Duke Energy Saver Program. The board also addressed lease agreements and safety concerns in county buildings.

## Key Decisions

- Denied rezoning from AR to R10; approved rezoning from AR to R20.
- Approved courthouse HVAC replacement with Gupton Services for $87,262.
- Authorized participation in the Duke Energy Saver Program with a cost of $33,227.86.
- Approved revision to GRRO Kitchen Lease Agreement.
- Authorized proceeding with Head Start lease agreement at Eaton Johnson.
