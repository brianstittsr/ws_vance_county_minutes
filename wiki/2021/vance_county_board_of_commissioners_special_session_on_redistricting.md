# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Redistricting

**Date:** November 17, 2021  
**Year:** 2021  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special meeting to adopt a resolution and map for newly drawn electoral districts following the 2020 Census. The resolution aimed to address population inequalities among districts to ensure fair representation. The board unanimously approved the resolution, which will be effective for the 2022 elections.

## Key Decisions

- Adopted resolution and map redefining Vance County Commissioner Electoral Districts.

## Action Items

## Topics Discussed
