# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Meeting

**Date:** June 15, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to address public hearing items from the June 1 meeting and other urgent matters. Key decisions included denying an appeal, adopting the FY 2020-21 Water District Budget, approving budget amendments, and adopting the FY 2020-21 Budget Ordinance. The meeting also covered the authorization of year-end closeouts and the adoption of the FY2021-2025 Capital Improvements Plan.

## Key Decisions

- Denied appeal from Mr. Anthony Peace for property at 1001 Health Center Road
- Adopted FY 2020-21 Water District Budget
- Approved budget amendments #33 - #35
- Authorized year-end closeouts for FY 2019-20
- Approved applications to draw down lottery funds for school debt service
- Adopted FY2021-2025 Capital Improvements Plan
- Adopted FY 2020-21 Budget Ordinance
