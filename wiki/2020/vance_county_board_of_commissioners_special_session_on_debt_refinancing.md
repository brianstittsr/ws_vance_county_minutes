# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Debt Refinancing

**Date:** July 21, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss and approve the refinancing of county debt. The meeting included a public hearing, although no public comments were made. The board reviewed and approved a resolution to refinance existing debt through an agreement with JP Morgan Chase Bank, N.A. This decision aims to provide cost savings and improve financial management for the county.

## Key Decisions

- Approved resolution for refinancing county debt
- Accepted proposal from JP Morgan Chase Bank, N.A.

## Action Items

- [ ] File application with the Local Government Commission for approval
- [ ] Execute and deliver the refinancing agreement and related documents

## Topics Discussed

- Refinancing of county debt
- Approval of shared parking agreement with Henderson Institute

## Budget & Financial Matters

- Refinancing agreement not to exceed $8,000,000
- Interest rate not to exceed 1.37% per annum

## Ordinances & Resolutions

- Resolution for refinancing county debt
