# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting on Rezoning Request

**Date:** July 8, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners reconvened its meeting on July 8, 2020, to address a rezoning request for a parcel located at 12996 NC Hwy 39 North. The meeting was attended by Chairman Gordon Wilder, Vice-Chairman Dan Brummitt, and Commissioners Thomas S. Hester, Jr., and Archie B. Taylor, Jr. The board unanimously approved the rezoning request, aligning it with the county's land use plan to focus commercial development in designated areas. The meeting concluded with no further business.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the rezoning request for the parcel at 12996 NC Hwy 39 North.
