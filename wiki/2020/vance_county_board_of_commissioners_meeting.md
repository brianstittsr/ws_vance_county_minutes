# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting

**Date:** September 8, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular meeting to discuss various county matters. Key topics included appointments to the ABC Board, recognition of the EMS team's achievements, and updates on COVID-19 relief funding. The board also revised policies for appointments to county boards and discussed funding requests for McGregor Hall.

## Key Decisions

- Appointed Dr. Rodwell Drake, Jr. to the Vance County ABC Board.
- Approved the revised policy for county appointments allowing non-citizens to serve.
- Allocated $50,000 in CRF funds to McGregor Hall.
