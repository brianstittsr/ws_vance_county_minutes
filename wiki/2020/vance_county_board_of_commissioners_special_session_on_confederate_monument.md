# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session on Confederate Monument

**Date:** June 30, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss an attorney-client privileged matter, approve previous closed session minutes, and address the disposition of a Confederate monument. The board voted to remove the monument due to public safety concerns, despite some commissioners expressing legal reservations. The meeting concluded with a decision to store the monument until further determination.

## Key Decisions

- Entered closed session for attorney-client privileged matter.
- Approved minutes of the March 9, 2020 closed session.
- Voted to remove and store the Confederate monument due to public safety threats.

## Action Items

- [ ] County staff authorized to remove and store the Confederate monument.

## Topics Discussed

- Attorney-client privileged matter
- Approval of previous closed session minutes
- Disposition of Confederate monument
