# Vance County Board of Commissioners Meeting Summary

**Date:** November 2, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a regular session where they welcomed Dr. Richard Benson II as the new EMS Medical Director. The board addressed tax appeals, approved untimely exemption applications, and discussed the Henderson-Vance Industrial Park expansion. They also reviewed committee reports on public safety, human resources, and planning, approving several recommendations including a new 911 console system and amendments to the ambulance franchise ordinance. Financial matters included lottery fund applications and finance software replacement.

## Key Decisions

- Denied Kennametal's tax appeal.
- Approved untimely exemption applications.
- Approved final change order for Industrial Park expansion.
- Awarded contract for Zetron Max Dispatch Console System.
- Scheduled public hearing for ambulance ordinance amendments.
- Approved consolidation of white goods collection to four sites.
- Approved applications for lottery funds for school debt service.
- Authorized finance software replacement with Edmunds GovTech.
