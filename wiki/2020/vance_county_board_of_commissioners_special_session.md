# Vance County Board of Commissioners Special Session

**Date:** February 17, 2020  
**Year:** 2020  
**Type:** Board of Commissioners Meeting Minutes

## Summary

The Vance County Board of Commissioners held a special session to discuss and approve key projects and requests. The meeting focused on awarding a construction contract for the Henderson-Vance Industrial Park Phase III, addressing a salary adjustment request from the DSS, and approving overtime for the Sheriff's Office. Key financial adjustments were made to align with project budgets and staffing needs.

## Key Decisions

- Approved the memorandum of negotiation and awarded the construction contract for Henderson-Vance Industrial Park Phase III to ELJ, Inc. for $2,149,457.
- Approved a salary adjustment for a DSS employee to $49,008.
- Approved Budget Amendment #20 for Sheriff's Office overtime.
